Red Roses

`

Red roses have captured the minds and hearts of lovers across the globe for generations. Giving a single stem or bunch of red roses on your anniversary, first date, Valentines Day or just because is the perfect way to say, “I love you” when words are not enough. Our appreciation for the beauty and perfection of the red rose is well-documented throughout history, in art, literature, mythology and on our screens. From classical love stories like Romeo and Juliet to todays hit TV show, The Bachelor, the red rose is an undisputed and universal symbol of love. In this article, well look at the history and meaning of red roses to discover where the connection between romance and love originated and what it means today.
